Services

We provide multiple services including concrete work, asphalt, excavations and NBN

Concrete

Allism Construction services is a well-established construction company that has extensive experience in architectural concrete and formwork design in both the domestic and commercial sectors. Our company specialises in exposed aggregate, coloured and plain concrete driveways, plus pathways and entertaining areas; offering an extensive range of products. If you have a slab or garage slab that needs doing, we have a great crew to take on any project to a very high standard. House & Garage slabs require formwork and concrete pumps. We organize everything for the job and always work with the owner/manager to ensure that the project is planned and completed in time. If you’re looking for concreters in Melbourne that you can trust, we are here to help”

Asphalt Work

At Allism , we specialise in all forms of asphalt & civil construction, asphalt repairs and maintenance. To enhance your project we also supply & install associated edging at your request including brick, timber, bluestone, concrete (coloured or plain)

Excavations

We provide all types of bulk excavation, cut and fill earthmoving for domestic, commercial and industrial/government projects. We have all the plant and machinery for massive bulk excavations, including excavations, tippers and all the earthmoving plant your project needs. We have trained staff and estimators to assist in project management of your project, including estimating and calculating volumes, time frames and schedules to ensure your construction project runs smoothly and most importantly to budget. With our extensive knowledge of the Melbourne suburbs and all of Victoria, we can provide an all-round service, from digging to removing of spoil. Our trained staff and company put customer satisfaction first. Not only do we aim at finishing your project as soon as possible, but we also focus on providing a clean and safe job site whilst we are completing your project.”

Telecom Networks

ACS offers construction and installation of pit and pipe, rectification works, network relocation, exchanges, and lead in conduits. Our construction team specialise in telecommunications and are well versed in the current regulations. ACS has been building telecommunications networks for more than 10 years now. In recent times ACS has been involved in the roll out of the National Broadband Network into new estates.
